Franzy-avro 0.0.1

A Kafka Serializer/Deserializer supporting Avro, and an add-on for Franzy, a Clojure Kafka suite of libraries.

Installation

To install, add the following dependency to your project or build file:

[ymilky/franzy-avro "0.0.1"]
